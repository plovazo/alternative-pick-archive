This web tool works for PC only. Phase 1: Zombie Draft. Press the corresponding letter on your keyboard (A-R) to secretly draft a zombie. Press Backspace to undo your last pick. Press Enter to confirm your full deck.

Phase1, plants ban 1 zombie. Phase2, zombies ban 1 plant. Phase3, 1 random plant is given. Phase4, zombies pick x cards (x is 0-5) as desired, while the remaining is randomly selected. Phase5, plants pick the remaining 4 cards. Phase6, any remaining zombies are randomly picked.
